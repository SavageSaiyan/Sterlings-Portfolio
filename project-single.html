Rock Paper Scissors Game

Description

This is a web app that allows you to play rock paper scissors but with a twist! Play rps but street fighter style

each option replaced by signature fighting moves from the characters and each option has its own fighting animation

Pick the right fighting moves and beat your opponent! First to win 2 rounds wins the whole set! Good Luck!
